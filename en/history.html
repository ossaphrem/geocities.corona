On January 17th, 2020, an anonymous 4chan user coined the catchphrase "Corona-chan" in a /pol/ board thread, with more users using the phrase to refer to the Wuhan province Novel Coronavirus in the following days. On January 21st, an anonymous 4chan user posted a drawing of a young Asian woman dressed in a red qipao dress, holding a Chinese flag and a bottle of Corona beer in her hands, to the /pol/ board (post and image shown below.). The thread accumulated over 300 replies. Eventually, more anonymous users posted their versions of the character in the thread.

In the following days, the character received spread outside of 4chan, with the artworks being posted to Twitter and other websites. On January 22nd, content creator MR. OBVIOUS uploaded a video to his YouTube channel about the character which accumulated over 22,700 views within 24 hours.
